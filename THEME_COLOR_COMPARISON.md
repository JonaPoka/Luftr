## Visual Impact Summary

### Color Temperature
- **Before**: High contrast, very vibrant (electric blue, energetic purple)
- **After**: Refined and sophisticated, still vibrant but more balanced

### Professionalism
- **Before**: Energetic and bold
- **After**: Premium and modern with a tech-forward aesthetic

### Readability
- **Before**: Good contrast (E8E8E8 on dark)
- **After**: Improved contrast (ECEFF1 on dark) - better for extended use

### Brand Feel
- **Before**: Fitness-focused with high energy
- **After**: Premium fitness tech with modern sophistication

## Key Improvements

1. **More Refined Blues**: Shifted from electric blue to refined cyan-blue for a more sophisticated tech aesthetic
2. **Elegant Indigo**: Replaced energetic purple with elegant indigo for a premium feel
3. **Richer Blacks**: Updated backgrounds to ultra-modern rich blacks for depth
4. **Better Contrast**: Improved text colors (E8â†’EC in hex) for better readability
5. **Modern Accents**: Updated success/warning colors to contemporary, clean tones

## Design Philosophy

The new color palette moves from "energetic and bold" to "sophisticated and modern" while maintaining:
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Excellent accessibility (WCAG 2.1 compliant)
- Modern tech-forward aesthetic
- Premium, professional appearance
- Clean, easy-on-the-eyes dark mode experience

Perfect for a fitness app that wants to be taken seriously while still feeling modern and engaging.
